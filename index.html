More about Zach

I am a fourth generation jeweler turned software developer. I’ve spent the better part of a decade mastering the art of jewelry repair, starting with an apprenticeship in Houston, Texas in 2009. This process taught me a great deal about who I am and what gives me fulfillment. In search of a new challenge, I came across Nashville Software School. I had very little experience in the field but the more I researched the more I wanted to know.

A 12 month software bootcamp and working full-time is no joke, and with my lack of experience it was that much more difficult. Right away I knew I would have to be committed seven days a week, and I’d have to completely devote myself. I was all in.

2016 at NSS changed my life. I found what I want to do for the rest of my life. I am having a blast, learning all I can and harnessing the skills I have learned so far. I am striving to master the tools of software development as I did in jewelry repair, and I’m so excited to be entering this field at this time. I can’t wait to see where this is headed.
